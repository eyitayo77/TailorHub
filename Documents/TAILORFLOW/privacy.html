Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon account creation

Welcome to Tailor Hub. We built this platform to help tailors and fashion designers manage their businesses with ease. Protecting your data — and the data of your customers — is something we take seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

Short version: We collect only what we need to run your account. We never sell your data. Your customers' information stays yours — we have no right to it beyond storing it securely for you.

1. Information We Collect

When you create a Tailor Hub account, we collect:

  • Account information — your name, business name, email address, and password (stored encrypted)
  • Business data you enter — customer profiles, orders, measurements, payments, and appointments that you add to the platform
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use the app (pages visited, features used) to help us improve the product

We do not collect payment card details. We do not run ads. We do not track you across other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain your Tailor Hub account
  • Authenticate you when you log in
  • Display your business data back to you — orders, customers, payments etc.
  • Send important account emails (e.g. password resets) if needed
  • Improve and develop the platform based on how it is used

We will never use your data or your customers' data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Your Customers' Data

When you add a customer's name, phone number, measurements, or any other information to Tailor Hub, that data belongs to you and your customer — not to us. We are simply the secure storage layer.

You are responsible for making sure you have permission from your customers to store their personal information in a digital system. We recommend letting your customers know you use Tailor Hub to manage their orders.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. All connections to Tailor Hub are encrypted using HTTPS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

During our beta period, we are a small team working hard to keep your data safe. While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is 100% immune to risk. We will notify you promptly if any breach ever occurs that affects your account.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in these limited situations:

  • Service providers — third-party tools that help us run the platform (e.g. hosting), who are bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements — if we are required by law or a valid court order to disclose information

6. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete your data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download all the data stored in your account
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Ask us any questions about how your data is handled

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Cookies

Tailor Hub uses minimal, functional cookies only — to keep you logged in and remember your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Tailor Hub is designed for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will remove it.

10. Changes to This Policy

As Tailor Hub grows, we may update this Privacy Policy. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you by email or with a notice when you next log in.
