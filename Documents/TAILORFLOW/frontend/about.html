About us

Built for tailors.
By someone who gets it.

Tailor Hub was created to solve a real problem — talented tailors losing money and customers because of poor organisation, not poor skill.

The Problem We Saw

Across Nigeria and Africa, there are thousands of incredibly skilled tailors — people who can produce stunning garments that rival anything you'd find in any boutique in the world. But running the business side of things? That's where things fall apart.

Orders tracked in WhatsApp chats. Customer measurements written in notebooks that get lost. Payments collected without records. No way to know how much money came in last month. Customers calling repeatedly to ask if their clothes are ready, because there's no system to manage it.

This isn't a skill problem. It's a tools problem. And that's exactly what Tailor Hub is here to fix.

What We Built

Tailor Hub is a simple, beautiful business management platform designed specifically for tailors and fashion designers. From the moment you sign up, you can start adding customers, logging orders, recording payments, and scheduling fittings — all in one place, from any device.

We didn't build a generic business tool and slap a sewing icon on it. Every screen, every feature, every word on this platform was designed with the real tailoring workflow in mind.
