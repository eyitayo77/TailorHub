Built for how tailors
actually work

Every screen, every workflow designed with the real tailoring process in mind — from measuring a customer to collecting final payment.

Order Management
Track every garment from deposit to delivery. Set due dates, update statuses, and never lose an order again.
Customer Profiles
Save measurements, notes, and order history for every customer. No more scribbling on paper.
Payment Tracking
Log deposits and balances. Know exactly who has paid, who owes, and how much is outstanding.
Fitting Appointments
Schedule fittings and pickups. Keep your diary clear and your customers on time.
Business Analytics
See your top customers, best months, and revenue trends. Make smarter decisions with real data.
